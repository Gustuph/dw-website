Angus Webb

Author | Editor | Attempted Human

Angus Webb is an aspiring entrant into the industry of creative writing, working primarily in literary fiction and creative non-fiction. He also dabbles in genre fiction alongside his work with other authors as an editor.

With a background that fuses studies in psychology with a qualification in writing, Angus weaves stories dominated by complex characters and explores heavy themes of grief and loss. The focus of his work often emphasises the cost of these experiences and what it takes to continue on.

Angus also has an interest in digital writing, and some of his most recent work delves into that form. The pull toward this area is the immersion of the reader, enabled through interactivity and choice-making. This allows for a more powerful entrance into the story world, meaning he can bring the audience on a more thorough investigation of the themes central to his work.
